Cross-component CLI: bond, pipe, invoke.

Implementation follows CROSS_COMPONENT_IMPLEMENTATION_PLAN. Phase A: bond flows (auth-to-web, oracle-to-chain); Phase B: pipe; Phase C: invoke.
